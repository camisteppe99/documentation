## Get Started in 4 Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [kelloggtickethub.com](https://kelloggtickethub.com) and verify your Kellogg email address to access all events.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your API key">
    Navigate to **Settings → API Keys** in your dashboard to generate your secret API key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse events">
    Use the [Events API](/api/events) or browse the dashboard to find available events and ticket types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Purchase tickets">
    Create a [Checkout Session](/api/checkout-sessions) to complete a secure, Stripe-powered ticket purchase.
  </Step>
</Steps>
