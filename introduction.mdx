Kellogg Ticket Hub is a purpose-built event ticketing platform designed exclusively for the Kellogg School of Management community. Whether you're attending a keynote lecture, alumni networking night, academic conference, or an annual gala, Kellogg Ticket Hub gives you a single, secure place to discover events, purchase tickets, and manage your orders — all powered by Stripe for fast, reliable payments.

## Who Uses Kellogg Ticket Hub?

Kellogg Ticket Hub serves everyone in the Kellogg community:

- **Students** — Browse and register for student-run events, club socials, and academic programming.
- **Alumni** — Stay connected by attending networking events, reunions, and signature Kellogg gatherings.
- **Faculty & Staff** — Manage event attendance and access order history for institutional events.
- **Guests** — Purchase tickets to public-facing Kellogg events with ease.

## Key Capabilities

Kellogg Ticket Hub brings together everything you need to participate in Kellogg events:

- **Browse events** — Explore upcoming lectures, conferences, networking nights, and galas from a single dashboard.
- **Purchase tickets** — Select ticket types, set quantities, and complete secure checkout powered by Stripe.
- **Manage orders** — View, download, and track all your ticket orders in one place.
- **Receive confirmations** — Get instant email confirmations with your ticket details and QR codes for entry.
- **Request refunds** — Submit and track refund requests directly from your order history.
